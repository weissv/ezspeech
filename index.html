Scope of the EZSpeech Digital Speech Therapy Application
EZSpeech is a Digital Speech Therapy Application facilitates the treatment of speech and language disorders,
voice productions, [swallowing functions] and general communication abilities that have been impaired as a
result of decease, injury, developmental delay or surgical procedures, without speech therapist being present
